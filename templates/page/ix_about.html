Mission Statement

These are the corporate objectives of Moncton Internet Exchange:

Benefiting New Brunswick's Internet community; Providing an electronic infrastructure to allow for the exchange of New Brunswick based Internet traffic among New Brunswick based ISPs (Internet Service Providers), NSPS (Network Service Providers), CSPS (Content Service Providers) and other Internet users; Providing New Brunswick residents with direct access to local internet content; and Increasing the transfer speed of Internet communications between New Brunswick companies, friends, neighbors and family members.
