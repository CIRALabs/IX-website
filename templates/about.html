Mission Statement

These are the corporate objectives of Saskatoon Internet Exchange Inc:

Benefiting Saskatchewan's Internet community; Providing, on a not-for-profit basis, an electronic infrastructure to allow for the exchange of Saskatchewan-based Internet traffic among Saskatchewan-based ISPs (Internet Service Providers), NSPS (Network Service Providers), CSPS (Content Service Providers) and other Internet users; Providing Saskatchewan residents with direct access to local internet content; and Increasing the transfer speed of Internet communications between Saskatchewan companies, friends, neighbors and family members.
